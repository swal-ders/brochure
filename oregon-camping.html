Oregon Canon Beach

Cannon Beach is one of the best known stops along the Oregon Coast. Less than 15 minutes away from the town of Seaside, lined with lovely nature views and lots of tourist shops. Enjoy yourself watching the sunrise and the sunsets on the beach with Haystack rock in the ocean, playing in the tidal pools, and watching the tide come in and out. The best way to experience Canon Beach, is to be there a week.
